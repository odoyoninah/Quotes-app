tiba mtaani history

TibaMtaani is a brainchild of four African youths who felt the need to bring about significant change in the healthcare sector. The search for a health facility to provide therapeutic aid can be a challenge but the greatest challenge is getting one that provides quality healthcare. TibaMtaani Web Application solves the two problems by providing a directory that lists health facilities in specific locations and their services. TibaMtaani team keenly scrutinizes the services provided by each health facility and once it satisfies the TibaMtaani standard, the health facility is made available on the web app
